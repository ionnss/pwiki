Linha 1: Psychology is the scientific study of mind and behavior.
Linha 2: Its subject matter includes the behavior of humans and nonhumans, 
Linha 3: both conscious and unconscious phenomena, 
Linha 4: and mental processes such as thoughts, feelings, and motives.
Linha 5: The word psychology derives from the Greek word psyche, for spirit or soul. 
Linha 6: The latter part of the word psychology derives from -λογία -logia, which means "study" or "research".
Linha 7: In 1890, William James defined psychology as "the science of mental life, both of its phenomena and their conditions."
